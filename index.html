Sushi Secret

Use the mouse to pick up pieces of sushi and place them in the trays provided.
Then place the tray onto the conveyor belt to send it through to the restuarant.
Happiness goes up if trays successfully go through with sushi on,
if sushi (or anything other item) goes through alone your customers lose happiness.

ABOUT

Sushi Secret is a game created by Jake Lyons and myself as part of Ludum Dare.
LudumDare40 had the theme of "The more you have, the worse it is.". We were inspired by an old Drake & Josh scene,
where the conveyor gets so fast it becomes increasingly difficult to package them up (spoilers).
Sushi Secret was our first Game Jam, and we are pretty happy with the ratings (65th in humor out of 1852 games).
